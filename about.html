monke

monke is a project that i originally developed as a typing game but has been diverted into a net art piece.

the objective is to type the randomly generated quote exactly the same as you see, as fast as you can.

there is a timer to track the speed that you are typing at and add a sense of urgency.

once the quote is correctly inputted, the typed quote is sent to the background and the next quote is generated.

once the wallpaper has five quotes, the textbox and the timer disappear to reveal the unique wall of quotes that you have typed.

a different wall will be created every time monke is run because the quotes are randomly selected.
